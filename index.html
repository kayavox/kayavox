Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to KayaVox. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy details how we handle the information collected from our landing page.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you explicitly provide, such as your email address when subscribing to the Collector List, downloading complimentary digital assets, or contacting us through the form.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

We utilize browser Local Storage to remember the items in your shopping cart and your preferred language. No third-party tracking scripts are active without your prior, explicit consent.

3. Data Security

Since KayaVox offers instant digital downloads, we do not process or store credit card details on our hosting server. Any email submissions for marketing lists are stored securely and used exclusively to send your purchased files or drop alerts.
